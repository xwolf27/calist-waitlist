Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

Overview

Calist is built on a simple principle: your data is yours. We are not an advertising business. We do not sell your personal information, share it with data brokers, or use it to train third-party AI models. We collect only what we need to run the service, and we are transparent about how we use it.

Information we collect

When you use Calist, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored hashed) when you create an account.
  • Content you create — calendar events, tasks, lists, notes, chat messages, and captures you add to the app.
  • Calendar data — when you connect a third-party calendar (Google, Outlook, Apple), we sync event data to display it alongside your Calist content. We do not store credentials; we use OAuth tokens that you can revoke at any time.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as features used and session frequency, to help us improve the product. This data is not linked to your identity for advertising purposes.
  • Device and technical data — IP address, device type, operating system, and app version, used for security and debugging.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Calist service.
  • Sync your data across your devices and with connected third-party calendars.
  • Send transactional emails such as account confirmation, password reset, and billing receipts.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your content to train AI models operated by third parties. AI features within Calist (such as Capture) process your data to provide the feature and do not retain it for model training.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us operate the service (hosting, payments, email delivery). They are contractually bound to use data only for the services they provide to us.
  • Other members of your household or team — content you share within a workspace is visible to the members of that workspace, as you would expect.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Calist and its users.
  • Business transfer — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may transfer to the successor entity. We will notify you before that happens.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored in a secure, encrypted database and backed up regularly. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. Sensitive fields such as saved passwords in Notes are encrypted at rest using per-workspace keys. We follow industry-standard security practices and conduct regular security reviews.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is purged from production systems within 7 days and from backups within 30 days. Some data may be retained longer where required by law.

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time from Settings within the app, or by emailing support@calist.au.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Export your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@calist.au.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and analyse how the service is used. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

Children's privacy

Calist is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or with a notice in the app before the changes take effect. Continued use of Calist after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at support@calist.au.
